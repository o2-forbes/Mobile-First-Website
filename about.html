ABOUT US

Mission Statement

At Heaven's Harvest, we are committed to combating food waste while providing nourishing meals to our community. Our mission is to create a sustainable restaurant and caf√© where everyone, regardless of financial means, can enjoy fresh and nutritious food.

Founders

Mindy and Mike, with a background in sustainable farming, have established a nonprofit organisation dedicated to reducing food waste in their local area. They are passionate about making a positive impact on their community and preserving dignity for individuals and families in need.

Restaurant Concept

Pay As You Feel
  • Our restaurant operates on a "Pay As You Feel" model, allowing customers to contribute what they can afford. No one is turned away, and everyone is welcome to enjoy a meal.
    • Daily Menu
  • We work closely with local supermarkets and distributors to create a daily menu using diverted fresh food and other foodstuffs that would otherwise go to waste. This ensures that our menu is both sustainable and diverse.
    • Volunteer Opportunities
  • In addition to monetary contributions, customers can also contribute their time and skills in exchange for food box collection. This allows individuals to support our mission in a way that feels comfortable for them.

    • Fresh Food Box Subscription

    Subscription Service
  • We offer a "Pay As You Feel" fresh fruit, veg, and cupboard goods box available for subscription. Customers can collect their boxes from our warehouse attached to the restaurant on select days.
    • Community Support
  • Our goal is to provide fresh, nutritional produce to struggling individuals and families while preserving their dignity and independence. Community hubs around the city help promote and distribute our food boxes to those in need.

    • Educational Outreach

    Changing Perceptions
  • We aim to educate our patrons about food waste and challenge misconceptions. Food waste is not just about rotten or expired food; it's about diverting perfectly good ingredients from landfills and creating meaningful meals.
    • Social Media Presence
  • We recognise the importance of social media in reaching our target audience. We are keen to leverage platforms like Instagram to promote our "Pay As You Feel" concept and engage with our community.

    • Mobile-First Design

    Accessibility
  • Our website is designed to look good and function seamlessly on both mobile devices and desktops. We want to ensure that everyone can access information about our restaurant and mission, regardless of their preferred device.
