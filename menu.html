Daily Changing Menu

Discover our Daily Changing Menu, where each dish is a testament to sustainability and creativity. Crafted from diverted fresh food and surplus ingredients, our menu evolves daily, offering a delightful array of options that combat food waste while satisfying your taste buds.

Experience the freedom of our 'Pay As You Feel' Pricing, where affordability meets generosity. Contribute what you can afford or what you feel each meal is worth, supporting our mission to provide access to nutritious meals for all.

Join us in championing a more sustainable future, one delicious meal at a time.

Seafood Saturday!

Special 1 - Prawn Ramen Delight

Plunge into a bowl of flavourful Prawn Ramen Delight, crafted from excess prawns and spare vegetables. Savour the abundent broth infused with spices, coupled with the tender sweetness of upcycled prawns and fresh, crunchy veggies. It's a comforting bowl of sustainability and taste in every slurp.

Special 2 - Ocean Harvest Salad

Dive into freshness with our Ocean Harvest Salad. Made from rescued Tilapia and surplus greens, it's a delicious celebration of sustainability. Enjoy the crispness of reinstated greens and the richness of repurposed salmon, all dressed in a tangy citrus herb dressing.
